MagicaVoxel to Mixamo to PlayCanvas

MagicaVoxel is a 3D building program that you can create almost anything with. I used it to create my character that very creativly called THICC BOY and he is shown leaping around. I build him in MagicaVoxel and then uploaded him to Mixamo which allowed me to animate him. From Mixamo I moved him to PlayCanvas which allowed me to show you THICC BOY in full animation.

High quality characters can be made with MagicaVoxel as seen above.
